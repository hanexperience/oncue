For trade businesses, video is the most powerful sales tool available — and the least used. A builder with a well-shot portfolio reel, a concreter with before-and-after job footage, or an electrician with a genuine customer testimonial has an enormous advantage over a competitor with nothing but a static website and a logo.

I produce video specifically for trade businesses — on site, in the real environment, showing the real work. Not a studio backdrop. Not a script. Real jobs, real outcomes, real people who vouch for your business in their own words. That's what builds the trust that converts a cold enquiry into a booked job.

Every tradie video package I produce includes multiple cuts from a single shoot: a website promo, social reels, before-and-afters and a testimonial. One shoot investment. Multiple assets across your website, GBP and social channels.

A customer who's comparing two tradies — one has real job footage and a client testimonial, one has a stock photo — will trust the one with the evidence before they've made a single call.

(02)  Why video matters for tradies

What video does that nothing else can.

Customers buying trade services are buying something they can't see in advance. Video closes that gap faster than any other content.

It shows quality before the quote

A homeowner hiring a builder can't see the quality of the work before it's done. But they can watch a 90-second reel and judge the calibre of your finished work. Video is the only way to demonstrate quality to a stranger before they've committed to getting a quote.

It builds trust with cold enquiries

A customer who found you on Google doesn't know you at all. A promo video and a customer testimonial turn a cold enquiry into a warm one. They arrive at the first call already sold on your quality — the conversation is about timing and price, not credibility.

It works across every touchpoint

Your website, Google Business Profile, Instagram, Facebook, Google Ads creative and recruitment ads all need video. A single shoot produces content for every channel — a website promo, social reels, a GBP cover video and a testimonial.

Most of your competitors have nothing

The vast majority of Melbourne trade businesses have no video at all. The bar is low. A properly produced promo video immediately differentiates you from every competitor who's still relying on phone photos of finished jobs posted as square images on Facebook.
