Why video works especially well for tradies

Text and photos can describe a job. Video shows it. For a trade business, that distinction is enormous — because your customer is trying to make a trust decision before they've ever met you.

  • Your work is visual. A concrete driveway, a new roof, a fabricated steel frame, a landscaped garden — these all look dramatically better on video than they do described in a paragraph. Video lets the quality speak for itself.
  • Your competitors don't have it. The overwhelming majority of Melbourne trade businesses have no video at all on their website. A single well-made job site video immediately distinguishes you from every text-only competitor in your local area.
  • Google rewards it. Pages with video see longer dwell times — visitors spend more time on the page — which is a positive signal to Google. A video on your homepage or service pages can improve rankings over time, not just conversion rates.
  • Recruitment is genuinely harder. Finding and keeping good tradespeople is one of the biggest operational challenges for Melbourne trade businesses right now. A short, authentic recruitment video — showing the team, the sites and the culture — will attract better applicants than any job board listing.

Common questions about tradie video production

How much does construction videography cost in Melbourne?

At Beets & Co, construction and tradie video projects are fixed-price. A half-day site shoot with editing typically starts from $2,000 AUD. Packages that include multiple deliverables — a promo video, social cuts, before-and-afters and a testimonial — are available from $3,500. You'll know the full cost before anything is booked.

Can you film on an active construction site?

Yes. We're used to working on active construction sites and industrial premises across Melbourne. We bring our own PPE, follow all site safety requirements and plan the shoot around your schedule to minimise disruption. The footage from a working site is always more compelling than anything shot in a controlled environment.

Do you produce recruitment videos for trade businesses?

Yes — and it's one of the highest-value things a trade business can produce right now. A short, authentic video showing what it's like to work for your business — the team, the sites, the equipment, the culture — will significantly outperform any text-only job ad. We produce recruitment videos for Melbourne trade businesses as part of our standard video packages.

What trade businesses do you work with?

Beets & Co works with builders, concreters, fabricators, plumbers, electricians, landscapers, equipment dealers, manufacturers and other trade operators across Greater Melbourne. If your business does real physical work and you want video that shows it clearly, we can help.
