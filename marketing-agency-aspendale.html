FAQ

Common questions.

How competitive is local SEO in Aspendale?

Very manageable. Aspendale is a small suburb with low keyword competition for most trade categories. A properly built page with correct structured data and a Google Business Profile listing updated for this suburb can rank in the Local Pack within 30–90 days.

What does a project cost?

Projects at Beets & Co start from $3,500. A full brand and website package sits between $7,000–$20,000 depending on scope. Fixed pricing — you know the number before committing.

I already have a website. Can you just do the SEO?

Yes — if your site has a solid technical foundation, I can do an SEO-only engagement focused on suburb pages, structured data, Google Business Profile and on-page optimisation. Get in touch and we'll look at what you've got.

What trade businesses do you work with?

Plumbers, electricians, builders, landscapers, HVAC, tilers, concreters, painters and similar trades. If you work in Aspendale and the surrounding Bayside suburbs, I understand your customer and how they search.
