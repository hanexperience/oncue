Why most digital marketing for tradies doesn't work

The most common failure mode is hiring a generalist agency that doesn't understand how a tradie's customer decides. They build a nice website that ranks for nothing, run Google Ads without suburb targeting, and produce social content that gets likes but no calls.

  • Wrong keywords. "Plumbing services" has thousands of competitors. "Plumber Dandenong" has a handful — and those are the searches from people who actually need someone right now.
  • Desktop-first websites. More than 70% of local service searches happen on mobile. A site that isn't fast and functional on a phone is losing jobs every day.
  • No review system. Google's Local Pack is won by review volume and recency as much as anything else. Most tradies have 6–10 reviews and no plan to grow them.
  • Chasing the wrong metrics. Impressions and follower counts don't pay the bills. Phone calls and quote requests do. Any digital marketing strategy for a tradie that isn't optimised toward those outcomes is optimised toward the wrong thing.
