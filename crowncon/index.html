The Brief

Quality builds. Invisible online.

Tim at Crowncon was delivering exceptional custom homes in the $800–900k range — confident, considered builds for people who wanted the real thing. The problem was that nothing in their marketing reflected it. Three different vendors, no unified direction, and a website that didn't come close to representing the quality of the work.

The brief was clear: consolidate everything, get the brand consistent, build a website that ranks, and create content that shows the quality that words alone can't capture.

The challenge
3 vendors, 0 direction
Marketing spend was scattered across a digital agency, a VA and paid ads — with no coherent strategy tying them together.
Target market
$800–900k builds
Quality-conscious homeowners who want a builder they trust. The marketing needed to feel considered and confident — not flashy or corporate.
Our approach
One roof, one voice
Consolidated everything — brand, web, content and video — under a single strategic lens, built around who Crowncon is actually selling to.
Website Design
