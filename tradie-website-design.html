(01)  What a properly built site includes

Five things every tradie site needs.

Most tradie websites fail not because of bad design — but because they're missing the basics that turn a visitor into a call.

01

Your phone number above the fold

A customer on their phone should be able to tap to call without scrolling. This single change lifts conversion more than any design update. If it's not the first thing they see, you're losing jobs.

02

Suburb-specific pages

A builder in Cheltenham and a builder in Frankston need different pages. Google serves local results. Your site needs dedicated pages for every suburb you target, each with real content about working in that area.

03

Fast load on mobile

Over 80% of local searches happen on phones. If your site takes 4 seconds to load on mobile, half your visitors have already left. Every site I build scores 90+ on Google PageSpeed on mobile.

04

Real trust signals

Google reviews displayed on your site, real job photos (not stock images), licence and insurance numbers, and customer names. Hesitant customers need evidence. A professional-looking site isn't enough on its own.

05

One clear call to action

Call now, or submit a quote request. Not both. Not three. Pick the action most likely to generate a conversation and make every page point to it. Confused visitors don't convert.

(03)  Why tradie sites don't convert

The five reasons sites don't ring phones.

These aren't design problems. They're structural problems — and they're fixable.

The phone number is buried

If a customer has to look for your number, they'll find a competitor who made it easier. On mobile, your number should be one tap away from the moment the page loads.

It's not mobile-friendly

Built to look good on a laptop, broken on a phone. Most local searches happen on mobile. If your site doesn't load and display correctly on a small screen, you're invisible to the majority of your market.

No suburb targeting

Google ranks local results based on relevance to the searcher's location. A generic page won't beat a competitor with dedicated suburb pages. You need pages that mention Cheltenham, Mentone, Mordialloc — specifically.

No social proof

A customer who doesn't know you needs a reason to trust you. Reviews, real job photos, client names. A slick website without evidence just looks like a front. Reviews are your most powerful conversion tool.

WordPress and plugin overload

WordPress sites built with page builders are slow, bloated and constantly require updates to stay secure. A hand-coded site has none of that overhead — and outperforms on every speed metric.
